Our Denver Story

Altitude-Tested Flowers

Founded in 2018, Bloomin' Mile High specializes in creating floral arrangements that thrive in Denver's unique climate. We work with local growers to source the freshest seasonal blooms that can withstand our mile-high conditions.

Each bouquet is designed with care by our team of expert florists who understand how to make flowers last in Colorado's dry climate.

Denver Flower Care Tips

To help your blooms last longer in our high-altitude climate:

  • Change water every 2 days
  • Keep away from direct sunlight and heating vents
  • Add a pinch of sugar to the water
  • Trim stems at an angle every few days
  • Mist petals lightly in dry weather
